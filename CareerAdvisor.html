Career Advisor Faculty of Information Technology

Our advisors specialize with helping students and alumni identify their interests, abilities, and values that coincide with majors at MEU and how they can relate to careers in their future. Advisors can assist with:

  1. Introducing the students to strategies for identifying possible career interests along with exploring interests and abilities.
  2. Self Awareness by dicussing students career goals
  3. Identifying opportunities by exploring what careers matter to gather career information
  4. Investigating graduate and professional school options by talking to Professionals in a student field, alumni, faculty and current students
  5. Developing a job search strategy being ready to meet to assist with self assessment, exploring career possibilities, researching careers or anything else related to student career development.
  6. Connecting with employers and preparing with mock interviews and workshops on best practices.
  7. Providing students an amount of valuable knowledge about building contacts and establishing professional networks
